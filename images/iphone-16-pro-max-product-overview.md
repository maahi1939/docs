<h1>iPhone 16 Pro Max – Product Overview</h1>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The <strong>iPhone 16 Pro Max</strong> is Apple’s flagship smartphone in the iPhone 16 series, delivering groundbreaking performance, professional-grade camera capabilities, and advanced AI integration. Designed for users who demand the highest performance and the most refined features, the iPhone 16 Pro Max combines sleek aesthetics with cutting-edge technology.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Key Features</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Display</strong>:<br>6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display<br>ProMotion technology with 120Hz refresh rate<br>Always-On Display with Dynamic Island</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Performance</strong>:<br>A18 Pro Bionic chip built on a 3nm process<br>Improved Neural Engine for on-device AI processing<br>Enhanced thermal performance for sustained high performance</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Camera System</strong>:<br>Triple-lens Pro camera system: Main (48MP), Ultra Wide (12MP), Telephoto (up to 6x optical zoom)<br>New AI-assisted photography features including Smart HDR 6 and Night mode enhancements<br>8K video recording with advanced stabilization</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Design</strong>:<br>Aerospace-grade titanium body<br>Available in four finishes: Graphite Black, Silver Titanium, Gold Titanium, and Pacific Blue<br>Slimmer bezels and refined edges for a modern look</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Battery and Charging</strong>:<br>All-day battery life with enhanced efficiency<br>Support for MagSafe and Qi2 wireless charging<br>USB-C port with USB 3 transfer speeds</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Software</strong>:<br>iOS 18 with enhanced customization, AI capabilities, and app privacy controls<br>Deeper integration with Vision Pro and other Apple ecosystem products</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
