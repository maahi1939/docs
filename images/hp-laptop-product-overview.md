<h2 class="wp-block-heading">HP Laptop – Product Overview</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>HP (Hewlett-Packard) Laptops</strong> are known for their wide range of models tailored to meet the needs of students, professionals, creators, and gamers. Offering a blend of performance, design, and value, HP laptops run on Windows and come with various configurations, including both Intel and AMD processors.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Key Features</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Performance Options</strong>:<br>Powered by the latest Intel Core (i3, i5, i7, i9) or AMD Ryzen (3, 5, 7, 9) processors<br>Options for integrated or dedicated graphics (Intel Iris Xe, AMD Radeon, NVIDIA GeForce)<br>SSD storage (up to 2TB) and up to 32GB RAM for fast multitasking</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Display</strong>:<br>Screen sizes from 13.3" to 17.3"<br>Full HD and 4K UHD display options<br>IPS panels with anti-glare and touch-screen models available</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Design &amp; Build</strong>:<br>Durable build quality with metal or plastic chassis<br>Ultra-slim and lightweight designs in models like the HP Pavilion and Spectre x360<br>Convertible 2-in-1 options with 360-degree hinges (Spectre and Envy series)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Battery Life</strong>:<br>Long-lasting batteries with fast charging support<br>Up to 10–12 hours of usage depending on the model and configuration<br>HP Fast Charge: 0 to 50% in ~45 minutes</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Connectivity &amp; Ports</strong>:<br>Multiple USB-A, USB-C, HDMI, and headphone jack options<br>Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.3 for modern wireless performance<br>Optional fingerprint reader and privacy camera shutter for enhanced security</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Software &amp; OS</strong>:<br>Pre-installed with <strong>Windows 11 Home or Pro</strong><br>HP Support Assistant for easy driver and firmware updates<br>Microsoft Office trial included on most models</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
